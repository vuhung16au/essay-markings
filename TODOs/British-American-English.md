Our app supports two modes: 

- British English
- American English

Which mode is being used should be determined automatic by our system based on users' essay (input).

The users should use one of the two modes consistently throughout their essay, and our system should provide feedback accordingly.
If they don't, we should provide feedback on that as well, and deduct marks for inconsistency in language use.

--- 

Pearson’s official scoring guide does **not** say that you must choose only British English or only American English in `Write Essay`, and I could not find an official Pearson rule saying mixed usage is automatically penalized.

What the official guide does say:
- For `Write Essay`, the spelling trait is scored simply as:
  - `2`: “Correct spelling”
  - `1`: “One spelling error”
  - `0`: “More than one spelling error”  
  Source: [PTE Academic Score Guide, July 2025, p.39](https://www.pearsonpte.com/ctf-assets/yqwtwibiobs4/WUcBAMkYCC9Dj5vs2HfVA/941d88d07ba7c2a5007f7ce1b18eedbf/Score_Guide__Test_Taker__-_PTE_Academic_-_July_2025__web_.pdf)
- The guide also says the published materials on Pearson’s site are the official information source. Source: [same guide, p.3](https://www.pearsonpte.com/ctf-assets/yqwtwibiobs4/WUcBAMkYCC9Dj5vs2HfVA/941d88d07ba7c2a5007f7ce1b18eedbf/Score_Guide__Test_Taker__-_PTE_Academic_-_July_2025__web_.pdf)

My best evidence-based conclusion:
- **Using valid British spelling or valid American spelling should not by itself lose marks.**
- **Mixing British and American spelling is probably not a direct spelling penalty** if the words are all correctly spelled forms.
- But **mixed usage may still make the writing feel less polished or less controlled**, which could indirectly affect human/AI judgment in broader writing-quality traits like coherence, linguistic range, or overall writing impression.

So in practice:
- `colour` and `color` are both likely acceptable
- `organise` and `organize` are both likely acceptable
- but switching back and forth in the same essay is still **not recommended**

Best advice:
- pick **one variety** and stay consistent throughout the essay
- consistency is safer, more professional, and avoids any chance of indirect quality penalties

One important caveat:
- I found the official Pearson scoring descriptors for spelling, but I did **not** find an official Pearson sentence explicitly saying “mixed British and American English is allowed” or “is penalized.” So the conclusion above is an inference from the official criteria, not a direct quoted policy.
