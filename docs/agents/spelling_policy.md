# Spelling Policy

## Rules

- Accept valid US, UK, Canadian, and Australian spellings.
- Penalize inconsistency when conventions are mixed within one essay.
- Use the vendored SCOWL-based dictionary in [data/words.txt](../../data/words.txt).
- Treat valid variants as acceptable by themselves.
- Explain mixed-convention deductions clearly in user-facing reports.

## Edge cases

- proper nouns may require special handling
- domain terms may require allowlisting
- contractions and hyphenated forms should be normalized carefully
