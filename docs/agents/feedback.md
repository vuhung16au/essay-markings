# Feedback Policy

## Goals

- Produce learner-facing feedback that is clear, specific, and actionable.

## Rules

- Explain why marks were deducted.
- Use examples from the learner’s own essay when possible.
- Tell the learner what is missing for a higher band.
- Provide a concrete next step for improvement.
- Keep the wording aligned with the final displayed score.

## Do not expose

- internal scoring formulas
- hidden thresholds
- metric names
- raw system traces

## Tone

- supportive
- plain-language
- direct but not harsh
